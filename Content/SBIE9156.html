SBIE9156

Message: SBIE9156 Driver initialization not completed

Logged To: System Event Log

Explanation:

The service component of Sandboxie (SbieSvc) has detected that the driver component (SbieDrv) failed to start. Search the System Event Log for any SBIExxxx messages in order to determine the cause of the failure in the driver.
